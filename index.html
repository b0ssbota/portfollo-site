About Me

I’m a dedicated Full-Stack Web Developer skilled in both Python/Django and WordPress/Elementor. I focus on building robust back-end systems while also creating intuitive and visually engaging front-ends.

Specifically, I work with:

  • Django for MVC architecture, user authentication, email integrations, and dynamic content.
  • MySQL and SQL for efficient, reliable database management and complex queries.
  • WordPress (Elementor) to quickly deliver flexible and user-friendly websites.
  • AWS for scalable, secure hosting solutions, whether it’s deploying Django apps or hosting custom WordPress sites.

I’m passionate about developing clean, maintainable code, ensuring site performance, and delivering a positive user experience. My projects range from membership-based fitness platforms to content-rich WordPress sites. Outside of coding, I enjoy learning about emerging tech, optimizing deployment pipelines, and collaborating on open-source initiatives.

Let’s connect if you need a versatile developer who can bridge back-end logic, front-end design, and efficient hosting on AWS!
